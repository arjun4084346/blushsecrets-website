Frequently Asked Questions

Threading, makeup & henna in Mountain View — your questions, answered.

How long does eyebrow threading last?

Threading removes hair from the root, so most clients enjoy clean, defined brows for about 3–4 weeks before a touch-up. Regrowth comes in soft and even, which makes upkeep easy.

Is eyebrow threading better than waxing?

Threading is gentler and more precise — no hot wax or harsh chemicals touch your skin, so it’s ideal for sensitive skin and for shaping crisp, natural brows. Threading is our specialty at the studio.

Does eyebrow threading hurt?

You’ll feel a light, quick sensation, but most clients find it very tolerable — and it’s over in minutes. Richa’s gentle technique keeps it comfortable, even for first-timers.

How much does eyebrow threading cost?

Brow threading starts at $15. Other areas — upper lip ($6), chin ($6), forehead ($10), full-face threading ($40) and more — are on our Services & Prices page.

How should I prep and care for my skin after threading?

Come with clean, product-free brows if you can. Afterward, avoid touching the area, heavy makeup, and direct sun for a few hours, and apply a soothing gel if you need it. That’s it!

Do you do bridal and event makeup?

Yes! Richa specializes in event, party, and bridal makeup, plus hair styling. Event makeup starts at $75 and bridal at $250 — book early for wedding dates. See the full makeup menu.

How long does henna (mehndi) last?

A fresh henna stain typically lasts 1–2 weeks, deepening over the first day or two. It’s perfect for weddings, festivals, and celebrations — and we also offer natural indigo hair application.

Where are you located and how do I book?

We’re inside Salon Finesse, 732 Villa St, downtown Mountain View (also serving Los Altos & Sunnyvale). Book online anytime, or call or text 408-314-3670.
