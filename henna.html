How long does henna last?

A fresh henna stain typically lasts 1–2 weeks. The color starts out light and deepens over the first 24–48 hours as it settles into the skin, reaching a rich reddish-brown before it gently fades. To get the longest, darkest result, leave the paste on as long as you can, keep the area warm, and avoid water and soap on the design for the first several hours. You’ll find more tips on our FAQ page.
