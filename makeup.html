Weddings, parties & special days

Bridal & Event Makeup in Mountain View

Event, party & bridal makeup plus hair styling — a flawless, long-wear look crafted by Richa in downtown Mountain View.

From a first date to your wedding day, the right makeup helps you feel completely like yourself — only a little more radiant. At Eyebrow Threading & Makeup, solo artist Richa Rawat creates event, party, and bridal looks (with optional hair styling) inside Salon Finesse on Villa Street, serving brides and clients across Mountain View and the greater Santa Clara County.

Event & party makeup for any occasion

A night on the town, a corporate function, a birthday, an engagement shoot, or a festival — event makeup starts at $75 and is tailored to the look you want, whether that’s soft and natural or bold and glamorous. Prefer to focus on the eyes? Eye makeup is $50, eye makeup with lash application is $65, and playful glittery eyes are $55. Add polished hair styling with straightening from $40 or curls from $50 to finish the look.

Wedding & bridal makeup in Mountain View

Your wedding is the day you’ll look back on for a lifetime, and every detail matters. Bridal makeup starts at $250 and is designed around you — your outfit, your skin tone, and the mood of your celebration, from a classic Western bridal glow to a rich, traditional South Asian look. Richa also styles hair for the big day, so your whole look is handled in one calm, unhurried appointment.

Trials, consultations & the day-of experience

For brides, the process starts with a friendly consultation and, when you’d like one, a trial run before the wedding so there are no surprises on the morning of. On the day itself, Richa focuses on a smooth, comfortable application and a long-wear finish that lasts through the ceremony, the photos, and the dancing. You can browse recent bridal and event looks on the portfolio to help picture your own.

Cruelty-free, quality products

Every look is created with high-quality, cruelty-free products — never tested on animals, and free of ingredients such as collagen, gelatin, and albumen. Gentle on sensitive skin and built to photograph beautifully, so you can relax and enjoy your event.

Book early for wedding dates

Wedding and peak-season dates fill up quickly, so it’s best to reserve as far ahead as you can — especially if you’d like a trial beforehand. You can see the full makeup menu and prices on the Services page, or find quick answers to common questions on the FAQ. When you’re ready, booking takes only a couple of taps.
