Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Eyebrow Threading & Makeup ("we," "us," or "our"), located in Mountain View, California, collects, uses, and protects information about our customers. By using our services or providing your information to us, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, mobile phone number, email address, and appointment details, when you book an appointment with us in person or through our online booking page.

How we use your information

We use your information to schedule and manage appointments, provide our services, respond to your requests, and — where you have consented — to send you appointment reminders and messages inviting you to book your next appointment. We do not sell your personal information.

SMS/Text messaging

If you provide your mobile number and consent, we may send you appointment-related and rebooking reminder text messages. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, and you can get help by replying HELP. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of any purchase.

We do not share, sell, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number, opt-in data, or messaging consent information to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information we collect is used solely to operate our business and provide the messaging service you signed up for. If we use service providers (for example, a messaging provider) to help deliver these messages, they are permitted to use the information only to provide that service to us and not for their own marketing.

How we protect your information

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Booking and payment processing are handled through Square, and messaging is handled through our messaging provider.

Your choices

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. You may request that we update or delete your personal information by contacting us at the details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
